MAZDOCKINS Taragiri Commissioned3 Apr 2026

INS Taragiri Commissioned into Indian Navy — Mazagon Dock's 4th Project 17A Stealth Frigate

Defence Minister Rajnath Singh commissioned INS Taragiri (F41) at Naval Dockyard, Visakhapatnam on 3 Apr 2026. The 6,670-tonne stealth frigate — 4th in the Nilgiri-class Project 17A series — was designed by the Navy's Warship Design Bureau and built by Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders (MDL). Over 75% indigenous content with 200+ MSMEs contributing. Features: reduced radar cross-section, CODOG propulsion, surface-to-surface missiles, medium-range SAMs, and anti-submarine warfare systems. Total programme: 7 frigates (4 MDL, 3 GRSE). A landmark for India's "Make in India" defence manufacturing.
