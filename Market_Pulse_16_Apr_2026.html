WIPROQ4 Mixed + ₹15,000 Cr BUYBACK16 Apr 2026

Wipro Q4: Revenue ₹24,236 Cr (+7.7% YoY), PAT ₹3,500 Cr (-1.9% YoY) — But Board Drops ₹15,000 Cr Buyback Bomb at ₹250/Share

Q4 FY26: Revenue ₹24,236 Cr (+7.7% YoY, +2.9% QoQ). IT services revenue $2.65B (+0.6% QoQ, +2.1% YoY). Net profit ₹3,500 Cr (-1.9% YoY), +12.3% QoQ. Operating margin 17.3% (-0.3% QoQ). EPS ₹3.34.
Deal wins: Total bookings $3.45B (+3.2% QoQ). Large deal bookings $1.44B (+65.1% QoQ) — sharp jump. Olam $1B deal referenced as flagship.
FY26 Full Year: Revenue ₹92,620 Cr (+4%). Net profit ₹13,200 Cr (+0.5%). Total bookings $16.4B (+14%). Large deals $7.8B (+45.4%).
THE BUYBACK: Board approved buyback of up to 60 Cr shares (5.7% of equity) at ₹250/share for aggregate ₹15,000 Cr. First buyback in 5 years. At ₹250 vs CMP ~₹210, that's an ~19% premium. Signals management confidence despite soft guidance.
Guidance: Q1 FY27 IT services revenue $2,597–2,651M → -2% to 0% CC sequentially. Cautious. CEO Pallia: "Pivoting to a services-as-a-software model through AI Native Business & Platforms unit."
Pain point: 3 of 5 verticals declined — Energy -5.9%, BFSI -0.5%. Estée Lauder client headwind noted. ADR fell 2.2% post-results.
