Sector Signal — Auto FY26 Was a Record Year Across the Board

The numbers tell a definitive story: Maruti (24.2L, +8.4%), TVS (highest-ever, +25% in Mar), Hero (64.7L, VIDA EV +70% MoM), Tata PV (+29% in Mar), M&M (99,969 in Mar, +21%), Hyundai (record Q4), Royal Enfield (12L+ record). The PV industry is heading to ~47 lakh units — a record. Key themes: UV/SUV dominance (Maruti UVs +17%, M&M SUVs +25%), EV acceleration (TVS EV +44%, VIDA +70%), and export surge (Maruti exports +43%). The only soft spot is Middle East-dependent exports (Force Motors -22%). FY27 starts from a high base — watch for price hikes (most OEMs announced 1-2% increases from April 1) and how monsoon shapes rural 2W/tractor demand.
