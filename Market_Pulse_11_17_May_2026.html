HFCL₹230 Cr Defence CapexTwin Order Wins11, 14 & 16 May 2026

HFCL formally enters explosive ordnance manufacturing — ₹230 Cr defence facility in Andhra Pradesh approved alongside two telecom order wins

HFCL's Board on 14 May approved establishment of a ₹230 Cr Defence Manufacturing Facility in Sri Satya Sai District, Andhra Pradesh, for manufacturing Multi-Mode Hand Grenades (MMHG) and similar products. Capacity addition ~40 lakh units; commissioning by December 2027; financed via internal accruals, debt, and proceeds from the previously-announced preferential issue of convertible warrants (₹555 Cr promoter commitment at ₹74/share). This continues from the company's 11 Sep 2025 defence intent announcement and marks the formal launch of explosive-ordnance production capability — adding to existing fuze/wire-harness defence work.

Bracketing the capex were twin order wins filed under "Bagging/Receiving of orders/contracts" — one on 11 May, another on 16 May (likely BSNL/BharatNet-adjacent given the recent rhythm). Backdrop: order book ₹21,206 Cr (all-time high) following March's $1.1bn / ₹10,159 Cr global OFC mega-order. Defence + aerospace order book ~₹2,230 Cr. Stock reaction was muted to negative on 14 May (-4.3% intraday to ₹146.80) — market may be weighing the ramp risk and customer-concentration on a maiden defence product. Important to track ordnance-customer signing in the next 6-12 months.
