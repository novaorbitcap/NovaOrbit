LICI1:1 Bonus Issue — Historic First13 Apr 2026

LIC Approves 1:1 Bonus Issue — First Ever in 69-Year History; 63.24 Crore New Shares Worth ₹6,325 Cr

In a landmark corporate action, Life Insurance Corporation of India — India's largest institutional investor and insurer — approved its first-ever bonus share issue at a 1:1 ratio. Shareholders will receive one free fully paid-up share (₹10 face value) for every share held on the record date (to be announced). Total new shares: ~63.24 Cr equity shares worth approximately ₹6,324.99 Cr, issued by capitalizing reserves and surplus as of 31 Dec 2025. Shares to be credited within 2 months (by 12 Jun 2026). Stock jumped 8% on the announcement anticipation. A press release with further details was also filed. For a company of LIC's scale (₹6 lakh Cr+ market cap, manages ₹45 lakh Cr+ in assets), this is a watershed moment signaling management's confidence in sustainable earnings power and commitment to shareholder returns.

Coforge Secures All Global Regulatory Approvals for $2.35B Encora Acquisition — Closing by End April

Coforge announced that all regulatory and statutory approvals across jurisdictions have been unconditionally secured for the Encora acquisition. The deal — the largest AI/ER&D acquisition by an Indian IT firm — will close by end April 2026. Key deal metrics: Enterprise value $2.35B. Funded via preferential allotment of 9.38 Cr shares at ₹1,815.91/share (vs current market ~₹1,223). Encora shareholders will hold ~20% of expanded Coforge. Combined entity: ~$2.5B revenue with $2B from AI-led engineering + cloud + data. EBIT margin target: 14%. G&A cost reduction: 20-25%. Integration planning on track; all key leaders retained. CEO Sudhir Singh: "Every element of anticipated synergies is on course." This is the culmination of a thread we've tracked since Ed #6 — Coforge's transformation into an AI-native engineering powerhouse is now one board meeting away from completion.
