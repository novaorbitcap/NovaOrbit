BHARTIARTL $1 Billion Raise 30 Mar 2026

Bharti Airtel's Nxtra Data Raises $1 Billion at $3.1B Valuation — Alpha Wave, Carlyle, Anchorage Back AI Data Centre Push

The biggest corporate action of the day. Bharti Airtel announced a $1 billion investment into its data centre subsidiary Nxtra Data, led by Alpha Wave Global ($435M), Carlyle ($240M), and Anchorage Capital ($35M), with Airtel contributing ~$290M. Post-money valuation: ~$3.1 billion. Airtel retains controlling stake. Nxtra will scale from 300 MW to 1 GW capacity, targeting ~25% of India's DC market. It operates 14 large core DCs and 120+ edge facilities. Recently partnered with Google for a gigawatt-scale AI DC campus backed by $15B. Alpha Wave — the firm behind Anthropic and OpenAI investments — leading this round signals serious global AI infrastructure conviction in India.

TEJASNET BharatNet Milestone 30 Mar 2026

Tejas Networks Ships 17,000 IP/MPLS Routers for BharatNet Phase III — Major Execution Milestone

Tata Group's Tejas Networks announced cumulative shipment of 17,000+ TJ1400 routers for BharatNet Phase III, deployed across 9 states and 5 UTs with live traffic at multiple locations. Total order: ~60,000 routers across 55,000+ Gram Panchayats. Tejas won 7 of 12 BharatNet III packages — the largest supplier. This is a critical execution proof-point for a company still reporting losses (₹197 Cr in Q3) while waiting on BSNL 4G add-on orders.
