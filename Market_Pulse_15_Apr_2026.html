Sector Signal — "Captive Renewable" as the New Normal for Large Manufacturers

The 26% stake pattern — JK Tyre (now 5+ solar SPVs), JK Cement (Mehrauni), SAMHI (2 Clean Max SPVs), countless others — is becoming the default compliance structure for captive renewable power under Indian Electricity laws. Expect this to be the template across manufacturing: 26% equity → long-term PPA → RE100 pathway. Companies benefiting include Roofsol, Oriana Power, Fourth Partner, Clean Max, STTY RE. A structural demand driver for India's rooftop/group-captive solar EPC industry.
