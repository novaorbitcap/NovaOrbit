Sector Signal — Adani Cement Empire Now India's Largest

With Penna now absorbed, the Adani cement empire's consolidation scorecard reads: Ambuja + ACC + Sanghi (NCLT approved Feb 2026) + Orient Cement (board approved) + Penna (effective 10 Apr). Combined capacity is pushing toward 140+ MTPA — making it India's largest cement group by capacity, ahead of UltraTech (~150 MTPA). The penalty orders filed by both Ambuja and ACC on the same day suggest regulatory scrutiny of this rapid consolidation.
